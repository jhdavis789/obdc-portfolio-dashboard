Portfolio Stress Testing

Apply spread shocks by sector to estimate “fair value” impact on the levered portfolio. Gross portfolio losses are amplified by BDC leverage (debt/equity). Spread duration approximation: for each +100bps of spread widening, fair value declines by ~(spread_duration × 0.01) of par. Most holdings are floating-rate (SOFR + spread) — rate duration is ~0.25yr but spread duration is ~3yr because the spread is contractually fixed. Results show both gross (asset-level) and levered (NAV/equity) impact, plus asset coverage ratio breach warnings.
