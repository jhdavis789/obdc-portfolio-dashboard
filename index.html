Portfolio Stress Testing

Apply spread shocks by sector to estimate “fair value” impact on the levered portfolio. Gross portfolio losses are amplified by BDC leverage (debt/equity). Duration-based approximation: for each +100bps of spread widening, “fair value” declines by ~(duration × 0.01) of par. Results show both gross (asset-level) and levered (NAV/equity) impact, plus asset coverage ratio breach warnings.
